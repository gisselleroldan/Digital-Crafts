All about Snowboarding

Snowboarding is one of my favorite sports. It's fun, a good workout, scenic, and exciting. It can be pretty tough when you're first starting out but once you can get going without falling every 5 seconds, it becomes way more fun.

First time snowboarding checklist

  • Snowboard and bindings
  • Snowboard boots and socks
  • Helmet
  • Waterproof jacket and pants
  • gloves
